Slider Setup

  1. Go to Slider → Add New Slide
  2. Slide Title: To insert slide title text by Slider Options
  3. Slide Subtitle: To insert slide subtitle text by Slider Options
  4. Slide Subtitle Link: To insert slide subtitle text link to scrolling page ex: #resume by Slider Options
  5. Slide Title Style: Bionick Slider has three types slide title style and they are Left | Right | Center
  6. Add the slide to a category by Categories
  7. Upload the slide image by Featured Image
